Services We offer

Minor surgeries

Minor surgeries procedures are defined as a set of proedures in which short surgical techniques are applied on superfical tissues, usually with local anestesia, and minimal complications that usually do not require post-operative resuscitation and need minimal equipment many of which are used on a daily basis.

Laboratory services

Laboratory services means services related to lab tests, including detailing information and specimen collection supplies for supported lab tests,lab tests requistioning and ordering, lab test specimen collection and accessioning, lab tests performed on specimens, recording of lab test results, delivery of lab test results.

Family planning

Family planning helps protect women from any health risks that may occur before, during or after childbirth. These include high blood pressure, Gestational diabetes, infections, miscarriage and stillbirth.
